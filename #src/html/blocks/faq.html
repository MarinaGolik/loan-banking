What should I know ?

Requirements to the borrower

The borrower should have a stable income and a permanent workplace for at least 6 months

Our loan offer

The loan amount is max of € 75,000. The rates start from 3.75% per annum and don’t change for the loan term which is max 10 years. Just prove your permanent income and wait only 24 hours for preliminary examination results. We will send an email with the decision. Forget the bureaucratic procedures. A loan is issued within only 8 days starting from processing an application.

Commission or other obligations

To begin with, you must be of the legal age of majority if you want to get a mortgage in Italy. The second significant requirement is having a stable fixed income. Confirm it with an indefinite employment contract. You can apply online only. The request will be processed as soon as it is complete. The loan provider will get in touch with you in case some extra information to verify the data is needed.
